Our Story

At Jollof Central, we don't just cook rice — we recreate the magic of Nigerian celebrations. Our firewood-cooked jollof carries that distinctive smoky flavor you can only find at the best Lagos parties.

Located in the heart of Benin City's GRA district at 86 Adesuwa Road, we've built our reputation on one simple promise: every plate served is fresh, flavorful, and made with love.

Whether you're craving our signature Firewood Jollof, perfectly grilled chicken, or sweet fried plantain, we bring the party to your plate every single day.
